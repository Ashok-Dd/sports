Introduction

Sports include all forms of competitive physical activity or games which, through casual or organized participation, aim to use, maintain, or improve physical ability and skills while providing enjoyment to participants and, in some cases, entertainment to spectators.

History

The history of sports extends back to the Ancient world. The oldest sport, which has been documented from ancient times, is wrestling. Sports became such a prominent part of the cultural landscape that the ancient Greeks created the Olympics, which were held every four years in Ancient Greece from 776 BC through 393 AD.

Types of Sports

There are many types of sports, including:

  • Team Sports (e.g., Football, Basketball)
  • Individual Sports (e.g., Tennis, Golf)
  • Combat Sports (e.g., Boxing, Mixed Martial Arts)
  • Water Sports (e.g., Swimming, Diving)
  • Winter Sports (e.g., Skiing, Ice Hockey)

Popular Sports

Some of the most popular sports worldwide include:

    • Soccer
    • Cricket
    • Basket ball
    • Hockey
    • Badminton
